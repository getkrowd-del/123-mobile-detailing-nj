WHY TINT?

ENGINEERED SHADE BENEFITS

More than basic styling aesthetics — professional carbon and ceramic automotive window tints offer vital vehicle preservation specs.

UV Blockade

Filters out 99%+ of dangerous solar UV rays, effectively preserving dashboard leathers and preventing interior fade cracking.

Thermal Rejection

Deflects up to 78% of raw solar heat. Keeps cabin interiors cooler on summer days, reducing overall AC wear metrics.

Cabin Privacy

Conceals internal contents from outside view blocks, increasing asset protection and security parameters when parked in urban lots.

Glare Reduction

Dampens blinding direct sunlight levels and nighttime high-beam distortions, reducing driver vision strain.
