OUR STORY

123 Auto Detailing started in a local driveway in Bergen County with a basic wash configuration, a premium microfiber array, and an uncompromised commitment to finish depth. We realized car owners in North Jersey deserved a precision fleet experience that shields clear coats right at their residency.

What began as target work quickly grew via word-of-mouth logistics. Today, we dispatch fully self-contained power and pure pressure washing units straight across Bergen County and Passaic County. Our mission matrix remains unchanged:

Deliver showroom-quality detailing parameters at your doorstep — with zero shortcuts and zero compromises.
